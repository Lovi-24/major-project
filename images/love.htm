Published On: June 13, 2019 08:00 AM NPT By: Diwakar Sapkota

The perplexed Love

I contemplate

The longing of our love

The gravity of it

Has turned feeble to attract

I feel all alone

In the mundane of worries

I am struggling with

The treachery of my life

The emotional odyssey of love

The plethora of lasting forever

The appetite for love

Has all turned temporary

The love is on the rock

It was never been circular

It was neither discordant

But could you condone my faults

The pheromone of your love

Still, get into my nerves

The spallation heart

Still beats for you.

 

 

 

Love, Perplexity, contemplate,
